A NEW GENERATION OF VINTAGE BIKE

When combined with Otero Upper, this trail is just crazy fast, flowy, amazingness. Drop in off a shallow rock slab and then whip through a few tight berms. Pretty dry and fast. Few sections rutted out but great experience for my first ride there! The end of the jump line trail there is a fairly steep climb to get to the next section.

VINTAGE OLIVA

Gets dry in the summer. The valley to your left drops away as you climb up and through a shallow waterfall. If you keep your momentum you can charge up pretty much all the climbs and the descents are steep.

LA BORIOSA

Dirt was pretty good, not too mushy yet, but the leaves on the trail are wet and very slick, a few corners were like riding on ice. A few wet spots, but almost non-existent mud.
